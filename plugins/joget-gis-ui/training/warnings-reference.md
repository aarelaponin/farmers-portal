# Warnings and Messages — Quick Reference

**Keep this sheet nearby. When you see a message on the map, find it here.**

---

## Green Messages (Success)

| Message | Meaning | What to Do |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Boundary captured successfully | Your boundary is valid and ready to save. | Save the form. |
| Corner X marked | A corner was recorded at your GPS position. | Keep walking to the next corner. |
| Map centered on [location] | The map moved to show the searched location. | Nothing — the map is now in the right place. |
| Connection restored | Internet is back. | Nothing — you can continue working. |

---

## Yellow Messages (Warnings)

These do **not** block you from saving. Check if the warning matters for your case.

| Message | Meaning | What to Do |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Area (X ha) is very small | The boundary area is smaller than expected. | Check if you missed some corners or if the area is correct. |
| Area (X ha) exceeds maximum | The boundary area is larger than expected. | Check if you added corners in the wrong places. |
| Corners: X/100 — approaching limit | You are using many corners. | Only add corners where the boundary changes direction. |
| Close to start point. Close Polygon | You walked back near where you started (Walk Mode). | Tap **Close Polygon** to finish, or keep walking if you have more corners. |
| GPS accuracy exceeds limit | GPS signal is not good enough to mark a corner. | Move to an open area. Wait for the GPS bar to turn green or yellow. |
| No internet connection | You lost internet. The map may not load new tiles. | You can still mark corners. Map tiles already loaded will still show. |
| Location not found | The search could not find the place you typed. | Try a different spelling or a nearby place name. |
| Fair — Wait if possible | GPS accuracy is okay but not great (within 10m). | Wait a moment for better accuracy, or mark the corner if you cannot wait. |

---

## Red Messages (Errors)

These **must be fixed** before you can save.

| Message | Meaning | What to Do |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Boundary lines are crossing | Two edges of your boundary cross each other. | **Draw Mode:** Click corners near the red crossing, press Delete, add new corners. Or click Redraw. **Walk Mode:** Tap Redraw and walk again in order. |
| Need at least X corners | You do not have enough corners to make a boundary. | Add more corners. You need at least 3. |
| Too many corners (X) | You went over the maximum number of corners. | Remove some corners. Use fewer corners on straight edges. |
| GPS not available | Your device does not have GPS or it is turned off. | Turn on GPS/Location in your phone settings. Or use Draw Mode instead. |
| Maximum corners reached | You cannot add more corners. | Delete some corners first, or simplify the boundary. |

## Orange Panel (Overlap Warning)

| What You See | Meaning | What to Do |
|--------------|---------|------------|
| **Overlap Detected** — with a list of records | Your boundary overlaps with one or more existing boundaries. | Look at the red areas on the map. Choose: **Adjust Boundary** to fix, or **Save Anyway** if the overlap is expected. |

### Reading the Overlap Panel

The panel shows a list of overlapping records. Each one shows:

- **Record name** or identifier
- **Overlap area** — how many hectares overlap (for example: 0.25 ha)
- **Overlap percentage** — how much of your boundary overlaps (for example: 15%)

The **red shapes on the map** show exactly where the overlap is.

## GPS Status Panel (Walk Mode)

The GPS panel is in the top-right corner during Walk Mode.

| Bar Colour | Accuracy     | Status Text                   | Action |
|------------|--------------|-------------------------------|--------|
| Green      | 3m or less   | Excellent — Ready to mark     | Mark corners now. |
| Green      | 5m or less   | Good — Ready to mark          | Mark corners now. |
| Yellow     | 10m or less  | Fair — Wait if possible       | Wait for better signal if you can. |
| Orange     | 20m or less  | Poor — Move to open area      | Move away from buildings and trees. Wait. |
| Red        | Over 20m     | Very poor — Check GPS settings | Check GPS is ON. Move to open area. Wait. |

---

## Network Status Indicator

Shown in the top-left corner of the map.

| Indicator | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| Green dot + "Online" | Internet connected. Everything works normally. |
| Red dot + "Offline" | No internet. Map tiles may not load. Corner marking still works. |

---

## Colour Guide for Map Shapes

| Colour on Map | What It Is |
|---------------|------------|
| **Blue shape** | Your boundary |
| **Green marker** | First corner of your boundary |
| **Blue markers** | Other corners of your boundary |
| **Red marker** | A corner you selected (ready to delete) |
| **Red lines** | Edges that cross each other (error) |
| **Red circles** | Points where edges cross (error) |
| **Red/dark red area** | Overlap with another boundary |
| **Grey outlines** | Nearby existing parcels (read-only) |
| **Small diamonds** | Midpoints — drag to add a new corner |
| **Dotted line** | Shows where the next edge will go (follows your mouse) |
| **Blue circle around marker** | GPS accuracy range for that corner (Walk Mode) |
